# Dataset licensing and access status

The MIT licence at repository root applies only to project code and original project documentation. It does **not** relicense the datasets.

## Zenodo imaging datasets

The three included Zenodo records specify **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)**. Preserve attribution to the original creators and cite the associated record/publication.

## NCBI GEO datasets

NCBI GEO provides public access to submitted research data, but the relevant accession pages do not state a single dataset-specific Creative Commons licence. Use is subject to NCBI policies, submitter rights, publication terms, and applicable human-subject safeguards. Cite the accession and associated publication.

## Dryad inflammation spreadsheet

The Dryad record identifies data files under CC0. The spreadsheet is not redistributed here because all authoritative anonymous retrieval routes tested on 2026-07-21 returned HTTP 403. No unofficial mirror was substituted.

## Governed resources

NSRR PI-ME/CFS sleep data, mapMECFS/searchMECFS, the UK ME/CFS Biobank, and You + ME Registry require registration, approval, a research proposal, a data-use agreement, non-commercial use, or provider review. They are listed in the manifest but are not downloaded.

See `documentation/manifest.csv`, `documentation/licences/`, and `documentation/citations.bib` for file-level details.
